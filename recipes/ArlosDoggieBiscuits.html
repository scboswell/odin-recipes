Arlo's Doggie Biscuits

Description

Celebrate your dog's birthday or just "treat" them to something delicious when you make these homemade doggie snacks!

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 c whole wheat flour
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 c peanut butter
  • 1 c water
  • 2 T honey

    • For the frosting

  • 2 T peanut butter
  • 1 T honey
  • 1/3 c cornstarch
  • 3-4 T hot water

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350F degrees
  2. Combine flour and egg. Add peanut butter, water, and honey
  3. Roll out dough on floured surface to 1/2 inch thickness and use cookie cutters to create shapes
  4. Bake for 18-20 minutes, until golden
  5. To make the frosting, combine the peanut butter and honey in a microwavable bowl and heat for 15 second increments, stirring in between until melted.
  6. Add water 1 T at a time until desired consistancy. Design biscuits as desired
