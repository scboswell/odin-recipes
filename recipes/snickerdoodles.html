Snickerdoodles

Description

This recipe will produce delicious melt-in-your-mouth Snickerdoodle cookies.
The Snickerdoodle is a popular Christmas-time cookie that will please all generations.

Ingredients

  • 1 c. unsalted butter
  • 1 1/2 c sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 t vanilla
  • 2 3/4 c flour
  • 1 1/2 t Cream of Tartar
  • 1/2 t baking soda
  • 1 t salt

    • Cinnamon Sugar Mixture

  • 1/4 c sugar
  • 1 1/2 T cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350F degrees
  2. Cream butter and sugar for 4-5 minutes until light and fluffy Add the eggs and vanilla then cream 1-2 minutes longer.
  3. Stir in flour, cream of tartar, baking soda and salt
  4. Stir together sugar and cinnamon
  5. Wrap dough and chill for 20-30 minutes, then roll into small balls and drop into the cinnamon-sugar mixture
  6. Bake for 9-11 minutes. Then let cool before removing from pan
